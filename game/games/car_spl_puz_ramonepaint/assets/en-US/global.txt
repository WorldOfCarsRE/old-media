Instructions
Use mouse to aim Paint Cannon.  Click to fire Paintball.  Stick 3 or more matching Paintballs together to remove them. Fill all paint buckets, and Ramone will paint the hood at end of round!
Special Paintballs
Paint Remover Ball
Removes Paintball it touches and nearby Paintballs of same color. However, also removes paint from bucket of that color.
Metallic Paint Ball
Flies in a straight line and removes all Paintballs in its way.
